If you want to install setups or new content directly from your browser, I’ve just made an extension! For sites like AC Club or Assetto-DB, it will add quick car, track, skin or app installation buttons, and for sites like RSR Live Timing or The Setup Market, buttons to install setups in a couple of clicks. Also, it allows to quickly start a race from RSR website as well.
